India

The Indian men's national cricket team represents India in international cricket. The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) manages the team. As of July 26, 2024, the team's captains are Rohit Sharma for Test and ODI matches, and Hardik Pandya for T20I matches. The coaches are:

Rahul Dravid: Head coach

Paras Mhambrey: Bowling coach

Vikram Rathour: Batting coach

T Dilip: Fielding coach

Australia

They are regarded as most successful cricket teams in the history of Cricket. The national team has played 866 Test matches, winning 414, losing 232, 218 draw and 2 Tie. As of May 2022, Australia is ranked first in the ICC Test Championship on 128 rating points.

More

England

As of 28 July 2024, England have played 1,074 Test matches, winning 395 and losing 324 (with 355 draws) . In the Test series against Australia, England play for The Ashes, one of the most famous trophies in all of sport, and they have won the urn on 32 occasions. England have also played 797 ODIs, winning 400.

More

South-Africa

The Proteas, currently ranked third in the ODIs, will be led by none other than the top One-Day Internationals batsman AB de Villiers. The team boasts eight players with previous World Cup experience in De Villiers, Hashim Amla, Faf du Plessis, JP Duminy, Wayne Parnell, Morne Morkel, Dale Steyn and Imran Tahir.

More

NewZland

New Zealand are the inaugural champions of WTC which they won in 2021 and they have also won ICC CT in 2000. They have played in the CWC final twice and the T20 WC final once.
